Albert Einstein

Any fool can know. The point is to understand.

Here's a time line of Albert Einstein life:

  • 1879- Born in Kingdom of Württemberg, German Empire.
  • 1896- He entered the Swiss Federal Polytechnic School in Zurich to be trained as a teacher in physics and mathematics.
  • 1901- He obtained his doctor’s degree.
  • 1908 - He was appointed Privatdozent in Berne.
  • 1909- He became Professor Extraordinary at Zurich.
  • 1911- He became Professor of Theoretical Physics at Prague, returning to Zurich in the following year to fill a similar post.
  • 1921- He received Nobel Prize .
  • 1940- He became a United States citizen.
  • 1955- Dies at the age of 76.
